## AI chatbot for application status 
An AI-powered Careers Assistant that answers candidate questions about roles, benefits, interview processes, and policies using a retrieval-based system with high accuracy and low hallucination risk.

## Live Links
## Frontend
https://ai-chatbot-application-status.vercel.app/
## Backend
https://ai-chatbot-application-status.onrender.com

## How to Use

1. Open the live link.

2. Ask questions such as:

“What’s the salary for Backend Engineer?”

“What are the Benefits & Perks at Vertex Labs?”

“What’s the salary range for Product Manager?”

"Vertex Labs Interview Process"

3. Click Interested to submit your email and preferred role.

4. Visit /admin to view conversation logs and most-asked questions.

No login required.
