Vertex Labs Interview Process

Our interview process is designed to evaluate skills, collaboration, and cultural alignment.

Step 1: Application Review
Our recruiting team reviews resumes for alignment with role qualifications.

Step 2: Recruiter Screen (30–45 minutes)
A conversation about experience, motivations, and expectations.

Step 3: Role-Specific Assessment
- Engineering: coding or system design exercise
- Product: case study
- Data: analytical exercise
- Design: portfolio review

Step 4: Panel Interviews (2–4 interviews)
Structured interviews with team members and cross-functional partners.

Step 5: Hiring Committee Review
Interviewers submit structured feedback using standardized evaluation rubrics.

Step 6: Offer & Compensation Discussion

Timeline
The full process typically takes 4–6 weeks.

Reapplication Policy
Candidates may reapply after 6 months if not selected.

Interview Accommodations
Vertex Labs provides reasonable accommodations upon request, including additional time, assistive tools, or scheduling flexibility.

AI Tools Policy
Candidates are expected to complete interviews independently unless otherwise specified.
